---
title: Topic Guides
---

Topic guides provide in-depth explanations of key concepts and
architectural decisions in Django ActivityPub Toolkit. These guides
help you understand why the toolkit works the way it does and how to
make informed design choices in your applications.


For new users, read the guides in order to build understanding
progressively. Experienced developers can jump to specific guides
based on their current needs. The guides assume familiarity with
Django but explain all ActivityPub and Linked Data concepts from first
principles.
